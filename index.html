Our History

Coffee shops have a rich history dating back centuries, evolving from the vibrant coffee houses of the Ottoman Empire to the European cafes of the 17th century.
In the 17th and 18th centuries, coffee houses became hubs for intellectual exchange and social interaction in cities like London, Paris, and Vienna.
They were frequented by artists, writers, philosophers, and businessmen, fostering lively discussions and debates.Our cafe shop has been maintaing the legacy from its 1920s and still continuing with its position with its own creativity in an enthusiatic mannner.
